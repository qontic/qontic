Q-Ontic Lab

Interactive quantum simulations and notebooks for comparing alternative representations of quantum mechanics.

Browser-based tools for exploring how different quantum representations describe the same physical phenomena. Apps support immediate exploration; notebooks combine explanation, context, and embedded simulations.

Comparative Apps

Standalone simulations that compare multiple quantum representations side by side.

Pilot-Wave Apps

Trajectory-focused simulations for exploring pilot-wave dynamics and wave-packet motion.

Interactive Notebooks

Longer explanatory pages that combine narrative, context, and embedded interactive demonstrations.

All Simulations and Notebooks

Complete catalog of currently available Q-Ontic tools.
